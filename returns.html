Return & Refund Policy

Last updated: January 2025

We want you to love your BLOM Cosmetics products! Because our items are professional-use nail and beauty products, certain guidelines apply to ensure safety and hygiene.

Eligible Returns

  • Returns or exchanges within 7 days of delivery if the product is unopened, unused, and in its original packaging.
  • If the item was received damaged, defective, or incorrect.

Non-Returnable Items

For hygiene and safety reasons, we cannot accept returns of:

  • Opened or used products (including gels, powders, primers, liquids, and brushes).
  • Items without original packaging or safety seals.
  • Clearance or promotional sale items.

Exchanges

If you receive a damaged or incorrect product, please contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos. We'll gladly replace the item at no additional cost.

Refunds

  • Approved refunds are processed to your original payment method within 7–10 business days.
  • Shipping fees are non-refundable unless the error was on our side.

How to Start a Return

  1. Email our support team at Shopblomcosmetics@gmail.com.
  2. Include your order number, photos (if applicable), and reason for return.
  3. Our team will provide return instructions.
