Our premium cuticle oil is formulated with a blend of vitamin E, soybean oil, and jojoba oil to nourish and moisturize your cuticles. The non-greasy formula absorbs quickly, leaving your cuticles soft and healthy-looking.

  1. 1 Apply a small amount to clean cuticles
  2. 2 Gently massage into cuticles and nail bed
  3. 3 Use daily for best results

Vitamin E Oil, Soybean Oil, Jojoba Oil, Natural Fragrance, Tocopherol (Vitamin E), Helianthus Annuus (Sunflower) Seed Oil.

How often should I use cuticle oil?

For best results, use daily. Apply after washing hands or whenever cuticles feel dry.

Is this safe for sensitive skin?

Yes, our formula is gentle and suitable for all skin types, including sensitive skin.
