Product Overview

Our Signature Gel Polish collection represents the pinnacle of professional nail care technology. Each bottle contains a carefully formulated blend of high-quality ingredients designed to deliver exceptional color payoff, superior durability, and easy application.

Key Benefits:

  • HEMA-free formula safe for sensitive clients
  • Self-leveling consistency for smooth application
  • Chip-resistant wear for up to 21 days
  • Compatible with both LED and UV lamps
  • Easy removal without damage to natural nails
  • Cruelty-free and vegan-friendly

Professional Use:

Ideal for professional nail technicians seeking reliable, high-performance products that deliver consistent results. Perfect for both salon services and retail to clients.

Application Instructions

  1. Prep: Push back cuticles and buff nail surface lightly
  2. Base: Apply a thin layer of BLOM Base Coat and cure for 60 seconds
  3. Color: Apply first thin coat of gel polish, cure for 60 seconds
  4. Second Coat: Apply second coat for full opacity, cure for 60 seconds
  5. Top Coat: Apply BLOM Top Coat and cure for 60 seconds
  6. Finish: Wipe with alcohol to remove sticky residue

Pro Tips:

  • Avoid getting polish on the skin or cuticle area
  • Cap the free edge for longer wear
  • Use thin coats for best results
  • Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight

Removal Process:

Soak nails in acetone for 10-15 minutes, then gently push off softened polish with a cuticle pusher. Do not force or peel off the polish.
