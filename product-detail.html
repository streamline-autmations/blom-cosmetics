Description

Our premium cuticle oil is specially formulated to nourish and strengthen your cuticles and nail beds. Made with natural ingredients and available in five delightful scents, this fast-absorbing formula provides deep hydration without leaving any greasy residue.

  3. Use daily for best results, especially before bedtime
